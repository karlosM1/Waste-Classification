WASTE

Proper Waste Disposal

Proper waste disposal minimizes pollution and protects the environment.

Reduce

Cut down on waste by choosing products with less packaging, buying only what you need, and opting for durable items. Small changes help save resources and reduce pollution.

Reuse

Give items a second life instead of throwing them away. Repurpose jars for storage, donate old clothes, or use both sides of paper. Reusing reduces the need for new products and helps the environment.

Recycle

Sort and clean recyclables like paper, glass, metal, and certain plastics. Recycling keeps materials out of landfills and turns waste into new products, conserving resources and reducing environmental impact.
